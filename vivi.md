<h2>ğŸ”„ How to Enable OTA Updates</h2>

<p>VIVI has a self-updating engine. To let it keep itself fresh, you need to grant one-time permission:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Open your device <strong>Settings</strong>.</li>
  <li>Go to <strong>Apps</strong> &gt; <strong>VIVI Music</strong>.</li>
  <li>Tap on <strong>Install unknown apps</strong>.</li>
  <li>Toggle <strong>ON</strong> "Allow from this source".</li>
  <li><strong>Done!</strong> VIVI will now download and install updates automatically.</li>
</ol>

<p>Not seeing VIVI on your car display? Follow these developer steps:</p>

<ol>

  <li>Tap the version number <strong>10 times</strong> to unlock Developer Mode.</li>
  <li>Tap the <strong>three-dot menu</strong> (top right) &gt; <strong>Developer settings</strong>.</li>
